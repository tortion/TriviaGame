Movie Trivia - Free popcorn

Game Instructions
Play for milk duds or a free popcorn refill
This trivia game is powered by OMDB and chooses a random set of Trivia questions based on the selected theme.
  • You will have 60 seconds (or ~6 seconds per question at the start of the game) to answer 10 questions.
  • If in your mind, "cats rule and dogs drool", try your luck on the 'Cats vs. Dogs' theme where you will be stuck with trivia questions pertaining to one or another.
  • Are you are a millenial? Try your hand at the 'Cowboy' theme, pardner. If you are an AARP member, try your luck at Pokemon movies!
